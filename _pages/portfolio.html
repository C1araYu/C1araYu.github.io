🐾 Meet 淘淘 (Tau Tau)

This page is for my lovely dog. She’s a cheerful and affectionate Shiba Inu, always wearing a warm and sweet smile. Her name is 淘淘, pronounced just like the mathematical symbol ττ (tau tau), which also means “naughty” in Chinese — a name that suits her perfectly!

淘淘 is already 4 years old, and I truly believe she’ll stay happy, healthy, and just the right amount of mischievous forever and ever.
